Kersten Odom

Hello! I am Kersten Odom, an aspiring graphic designer. I have been creating for as long as I remember with anything I can get my hands on. I love getting messy with all types of mediums but my emphasis has been on digital art for around four years now.

In addition to my passion for art, I enjoy karate, long walks, and almost anything active. I also was a teacher aide for ten years where I worked with Kindergarten - 8th grade students.
