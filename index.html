Kersten Odom

Hello! I am Kersten Odom, an aspiring graphic designer. I have been creating art for as long as I remember anything I can get my hands on. I love getting messy with all types of mediums, but my emphasis has been on digital art for around four years now.

I have spent several years doing graphic design and traditional artwork for several different clients. This artwork included large acrylic art pieces for indoor decor, greeting card designs that I both designed and printed, formatted and designed invitations, logo creations, and personal illustrations. I am experienced in many mediums including drawing, acrylic paint, watercolor paint, pastels, lettering, Procreate, Canva, Photoshop, and Illustrator.
